Text and images
With this idegaWeb Block you can display any text you want in the layout and style of your choice. It is designed to allow editing of all general material on web pages. With built in localization support you can write your text in any of the site's preferred languages. ÁTVR / Vínbúð
This summer Idega together with our business partner Strengur signed an agreement with the Alcohol & Tobacco Monopoly in Iceland to implement a new website.

The website has been accessable via the address www.vinbud.is, and now it has replaced the old www.atvr.is.

This new website demonstrates the flexibility and power of the idegaWeb product. Here we integrate idegaWeb Builder to both Intershop (e-commerce application) and Navision Attain (Accounting software). The total solution works seamlessly with a completely consistent interface.

The website is hosted by Strengur and it is running on Informix database.
